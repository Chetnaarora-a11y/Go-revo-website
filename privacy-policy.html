Privacy Policy

At Go Revo, owned and managed by PRIMETOWER INFRASTRUCTURE PRIVATE LIMITED, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website, submit an enquiry or interact with our advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Address or Pincode
  • Monthly Electricity Bill Details
  • Property Information
  • Information submitted through enquiry forms
  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Operating System
  • Device Information
  • Pages Visited
  • Cookies, Pixels and Advertising Identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Assess rooftop solar eligibility.
  • Provide quotations and subsidy information.
  • Schedule consultations and site visits.
  • Improve our products and services.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Measure advertising performance.
  • Prevent fraud and misuse.
  • Comply with applicable laws.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies, tracking pixels, tags and similar technologies to improve website functionality, analyse visitor behaviour and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

These technologies may collect information including browser details, device information, IP address, pages visited, time spent on the website and interactions with our website.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function properly.

4. Google Services

Our website uses Google services including Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager and Google Conversion Tracking.

These services help us analyse website traffic, measure campaign performance, improve user experience and provide relevant advertisements.

5. Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

We may use Meta Pixel and Meta Business Tools to measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns, understand visitor interactions and improve our marketing efforts.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information.

We may share limited information only with trusted service providers including:

  • Solar installation partners.
  • Government agencies for subsidy processing, where applicable.
  • Website hosting providers.
  • Google services.
  • Meta Business services.
  • CRM and customer support providers.
  • Authorities where required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, misuse or alteration.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Object to certain processing activities.

10. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated effective date.
